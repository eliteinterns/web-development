Welcome to Our Student Council

We are dedicated to enhancing student life and building a stronger school community through leadership, service, and advocacy.

About Us

The Student Council serves as the voice of the student body, working closely with faculty and administration to create positive changes within our school. We organize events, address student concerns, and promote school spirit.

Our mission is to foster a vibrant school community where every student feels valued and represented. Through dedicated service and leadership, we strive to make our school a better place for everyone.
