About Me

Hi, I'm Alex

I'm a passionate photographer and visual artist based in New York City. For over 10 years, I've been capturing moments and creating images that tell compelling stories.

My work explores the intersection between urban landscapes and human emotion. Through my lens, I aim to reveal the extraordinary in everyday scenes and challenge viewers to see the familiar in new ways.

I've exhibited my work in galleries across the country and collaborated with brands that share my vision for authentic storytelling. When I'm not behind the camera, I'm teaching photography workshops and mentoring emerging artists.
