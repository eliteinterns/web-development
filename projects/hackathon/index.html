CodeCraft is the premier hackathon event bringing together developers, designers, and innovators to solve real-world problems through technology.

This year's theme is "Sustainable Tech Solutions" - focusing on leveraging technology to address environmental challenges and promote sustainable practices.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to have a team to participate?

No, you can register as an individual and we'll help you find teammates during the team-formation session before the hackathon begins.

What should I bring to the hackathon?

Please bring your laptop, charger, any specific hardware you may need for your project, and your creativity! We'll provide food, beverages, WiFi, and a comfortable hacking space.

Is there a participation fee?

No, participation is completely free! Thanks to our sponsors, we're able to provide this opportunity at no cost to participants.

Do I need to be a programmer to participate?

Not necessarily! We welcome designers, project managers, and creative thinkers too. Successful teams often include members with diverse skill sets.

Will there be food and accommodations?

Yes, meals and snacks will be provided throughout the event. As for accommodation, we have special rates with nearby hotels, but participants are responsible for their own lodging arrangements.
