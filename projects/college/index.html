About Our Department

Founded in 1985, the Department of Computer Science has been at the forefront of computing education and research for over three decades. Our department is recognized nationally for our academic excellence and innovative research initiatives.

We pride ourselves on providing a supportive and challenging environment where students can develop the technical skills and creative thinking needed for success in the rapidly evolving field of computer science.

Our state-of-the-art facilities include specialized labs for cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and software development, providing students with hands-on experience using the latest technologies and tools.

Our mission is to advance the field of computer science through excellence in education, research, and innovation, preparing students to be leaders in the digital age.

We aim to be globally recognized as a hub for pioneering research and transformative education in computer science, driving technological advancements that benefit society.

Our department is guided by core values including innovation, integrity, collaboration, diversity, and excellence in all our academic and research endeavors.
