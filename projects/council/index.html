About Us

The Central High Student Council serves as the voice of our student body, working diligently to address concerns, plan events, and foster a sense of community and school spirit.

Founded in 1985, our council has a rich history of leadership and service. We meet weekly to discuss initiatives, plan events, and collaborate with administration to improve student life.

Our mission is to represent student interests, foster school spirit, organize engaging activities, and develop leadership skills among our peers.
